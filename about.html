OUR COMPANY OVERVIEW

Nzallacado Oil Company, established in 2016, is specialized in processing Avocado fruits and oil seed oils for both edible oils and oils for cosmetic use. In addition to this, the company is producing several products as by-product of the oils production, such as soaps, nutritious products in powder form. The founder of the company Twahir Nzallawahe is an expert in the agricultural industry with many years of experience and extensive knowledge in various fields including crops farming, oils extraction, marketing, etc.
Nzallacado Oil Company is the ONLY producer in Tanzania of edible oils such as Avocado Oil, Pumpkin seeds Oil and Moringa oil. These oils have very high nutritional value and offer great health benefits to the consumers.
Nzallacado Oil Company extracts the oils by using cold pressing as an extraction method, which fully preserves the quality and properties of the oils. Due to this, the products of Nzallacado Oil Company are high quality products of high nutritional value. As mentioned above Nzallacado oil company ltd plays pivotal role in providing market solution to Avocado growers and seed oil growers and hence, give them incomes.
